# Zwanski Watchdog — Ethical Covenant

Zwanski Watchdog is operated in alignment with responsible security research and
privacy law. All contributors, operators, and integrators agree to uphold the
following principles.

## 1. Lawful surface only

Scans target **publicly accessible** data and services only. No credential
stuffing, no bypass of access controls, and no testing against systems without
legal authorization.

## 2. Responsible disclosure

Findings are disclosed responsibly with a **minimum 72-hour private notice** to
the affected entity before any **public** record of the finding, except where
law or a binding order requires otherwise.

## 3. No sale of finding data

Finding data is **not sold** to third parties under any circumstances.

## 4. Equal access

Government and commercial subscribers receive the **same product capabilities**.
There are **no backdoors**, **no special surveillance features**, and **no bulk
identity data** products.

## 5. Researcher protection

Researcher identities are **protected from disclosure** to reported organizations
except where the researcher opts in or law requires disclosure.

## 6. No weaponization against individuals

There is **zero tolerance** for using findings to harass, stalk, or harm
individuals. PII-focused findings are handled with minimization and legal review.
